Every artist romanticises the deal — the advance, the rollout, the machine behind you. Dre Butterz has been on the other side of the glass long enough to know what that machine actually charges.

We pull apart the conversation: where the money really goes, what 'creative control' means once the ink dries, and how perception gets manufactured long before the music does.

The label era isn't dead — it just learned to hide the receipt.
